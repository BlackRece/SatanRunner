You are SATAN! You have to MOVE FORWARD along the Pope's train, evading obstacles to reach and defeat the Pope.

This was created in Unity in a team of five people for a game jam held by Staffordshire University to welcome new students.

The jam was judged by representatives from Codemasters that offered prizes to the wining team.

About Me

 

With a passion for games since a young age, I began persuing my dream of developing computer games in 2018.

I am currently taking my hobby of making games since the age of 11, to a professional level using the skills gained in my spare time and collating them to excell as a developer. I enjoy the logical process required to find solutions, resulting in enjoyable game-play.

I was a programmer on the wining team at a game jam held at Staffordshire University and have been motivated to participate in as many game jams as possible while studying full time.

I enjoy attending gaming exhibitions as they provide the opportunity to converse with other professionals in the industry. I share my appreciation for their efforts and learn from their views and experiences.
